Australia’s Flight Network

Explore air connectivity between Australia’s capital cities. Use the year slider to see how traffic changes. Line colour shows the total number of flights on each city-pair for the selected year.

About this view

What the map shows

Each line connects two capital cities. The colour intensity represents the total flights in that year (sum of monthly services). This highlights Australia’s busiest corridors—often the east-coast triangle (SYD–MEL–BNE).

Drag the slider to compare years. Notice dips in 2020 during the pandemic and recovery afterwards (as available in the data). Hover a route to see exact totals.

Data: BITRE city-pair statistics (filtered to the seven capitals).
